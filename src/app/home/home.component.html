Why Choose UIowa?

University of Iowa has been ranked among top 100 in USNews rankings in a range of disciplines such as Chemistry, Engineering, Mathematics, Computer Science, Business, Medicine, Fine Arts, Law etc. For more information, click here.

Irrespective of the discipline pursued, over 44% of University of Iowa graduates are able to get job offers even before gradaution while 37% graduates get jobs within first 3 months after graduation. For more information, click here.

There is a well-bonded Sri Lankan community located near University of Iowa, in the cities of Iowa City and Coralville with few established Sri Lankan families and a student community of over 20 Sri Lankan students in various disciplines. They gather regularly to share fun and love, be it a birthday, graduation or farewell.
